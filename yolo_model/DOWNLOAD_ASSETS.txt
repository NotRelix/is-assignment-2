**DOWNLOAD URL:**
The file was downloaded from the following Kaggle dataset:
https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/shivam316/yolov3-weights

Please click the "Download dataset as zip (231 MB)" button on that page, extract the 'yolov3.weights' file, and place it directly into this 'yolo_model/' folder.
